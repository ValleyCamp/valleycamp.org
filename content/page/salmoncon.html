Suggested Items to bring for Radio Activities

Given the fact that there is a tower and beam with rotator available for us, as well as tall trees with pulleys already installed, both near the group shelter and the RV camp ground, we will have the opportunity to get several portable stations on the air. There is a Triplexer also, which means that the beam can be used on 20, 15 and 10 at the same time.

Do bring either complete portable stations, antennas, favorite paddles, or any other radio related items that would help provide the variety of rigs, stations, and station equipment that we all enjoy seeing and trying out. 

There is discussion of the possibility of activating a SOTA station from a nearby peak. Stay tuned. 

TAKE NOTE: PNW QRP does not charge a fee for attending Salmoncon. 

However, because of the fact that Valley Camp does not charge us a fee for the use of the group shelter, including kitchen facilities, sound system, beam antenna, and so forth, we ask each attendee to make an appropriate donation directly to Valley Camp. A collection box will be available in the presentation area of the Group Shelter.
